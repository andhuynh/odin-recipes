Katsu

Description

This recipe is taken from the following:

Original Food Network Recipe

Katsu is one of my favorite dishes to eat. The crunchiness of the bread coating combined with the juiciness of the meat is the best treat to eat over a bowl of piping hot rice and some shredded cabbage for that extra crunch. A side of tonkatsu sauce adds that extra sweetness and tanginess the meat needs to balace out the flavors.

Ingredients:

  • meat of choice (chicken or pork)
  • flour
  • eggs
  • panko
  • salt and pepper
  • vegetable oil
  • cabbage
  • tonkatsu sauce

Steps

  1. Have 3 separate plates for flour, eggs, and panko
  2. Dip meat into flour, then egg, then panko, and set aside
  3. Fry meat in 350 degrees Fahrenheit oil, 3 minutes on each side
  4. Take out and serve with rice and cabbage with tonkatsu sauce on top
