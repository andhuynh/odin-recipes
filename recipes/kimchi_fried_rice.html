Kimchi Fried Rice

Description:

This recipe was taken from the following:

NY Times Cooking Recipe

An easy meal to cook up, kimchi fried rice will always have a special place in my heart. With a fried egg on top, this simple meal is a great dinner to cook when limited in time.

Ingredients:

  • kimchi
  • rice
  • spam
  • diced onion
  • green onion
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce
  • butter
  • vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Add butter to nonstick pan and add onion, kimchi and spam
  2. Add rice to the pan then drizzle sesame oil and soy sauce to taste
  3. In a seperate pan, add vegetable oil and fry eggs
  4. Plate fried rice with egg over it and top with green onion
